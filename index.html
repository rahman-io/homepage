🚀 Help Baby Pixel fly!

Type the words to lift Baby Pixel. Grab ⭐ coins, catch ✨ bonus words for a boost, and dodge ⛈️ storms by changing your height. The longer you fly, the higher the level!

Choose your level:
⌨️ Type = fly up · hold ↓/Shift or the Dive button = swoop down
🎮 How to play
  • Type the words shown to flap and rise — faster typing = higher flight.
  • Swoop down: hold ↓ or Shift (or press & hold the Dive button) to descend safely toward low coins or under storms.
  • ⭐ Coins give points, 🌟 gold ones give 2× score, ✨ bonus words grant a shield.
  • ⛈️ Storms: change your height to dodge them — a shield protects you.
  • Survive to climb levels — it speeds up over time!
