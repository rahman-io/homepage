About us

Useful ideas deserve to be shared.

REPLACE — Research Empowerment and Learning Centre Institute — is an institute working in research, empowerment, and learning. We hold to one principle: a useful idea deserves to be shared with the world, no matter how small its form. The value of a work comes not from how sophisticated or expensive it is, but from how many people it helps.
